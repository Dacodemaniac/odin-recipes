The Nigerian Jollof Rice Recipe

The famous Nigerian jollof Rice

This special Delicacy is prepared using different Recipes such as ; tomatoes, hot peppers, chevon e.t.c and has its origin from Nigeria which most times leads to a never ending battle for supremacy between the Ghanaians and Nigerians on who makes the better meal.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Hot peppers
  • chevon(not compuslory)
  • Onions
  • Vegetable oik
  • curry leafs
  • Chicken
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Parboil the Rice
  2. Boil,season and grill your chicken or chevon
  3. Blend the pepper and tomatoes till it becomes smooth
  4. Fry sauce for 6 minutes
  5. Add parboiled rice
  6. Season and stir together with a wooden spoon
  7. Allow to cook for about 40 minutes or till you get desired taste
  8. Serve and eat while warm
